QuizVerse uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. This policy explains how we use these technologies and how you can manage your preferences.

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help websites remember information about your visit.

QuizVerse uses cookies and similar technologies like:

  • Browser cookies - Small text files stored by your browser
  • LocalStorage - For larger amounts of data storage
  • SessionStorage - Temporary storage during your visit
