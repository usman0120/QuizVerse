Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

At QuizVerse, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

We collect minimal data to provide and improve our services:

  • Basic profile information - Only if you choose to create a profile (username, country, avatar)
  • User progress - Quiz scores, completed challenges, and earned badges
  • Usage data - Anonymous analytics about feature usage and quiz performance
  • Browser information - Device type, browser version for compatibility purposes

We do not collect personal identifiers like real names, addresses, or payment information.

All user data is stored locally in your browser:

  • LocalStorage - For user preferences and progress
  • IndexedDB - For larger datasets like quiz history
  • SessionStorage - For temporary data during your visit

We do not use cloud storage or servers to store your personal data. Clearing your browser data will reset your progress.

We respect your privacy and avoid invasive tracking:

  • No third-party trackers - We don't use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or similar services
  • Anonymous usage data - Optional aggregated statistics about feature usage (toggle below)
  • Cookie usage - Only for essential functions like maintaining login sessions
Anonymous Usage Analytics

Help us improve by sharing anonymous usage data. You can change this anytime.

We use cookies responsibly:

  • Essential cookies - Required for basic functionality like maintaining your streak
  • Preference cookies - Remember your theme choice and settings
  • No tracking cookies - We don't use cookies for advertising or profiling

You can manage cookie preferences on our Cookies Policy page.

QuizVerse is COPPA-compliant and safe for children:

  • No personal data collection from children under 13
  • Parental controls - Parents can reset local data by clearing browser storage
  • Educational focus - All content is appropriate for young learners
  • No targeted advertising - We don't serve ads or track children

If you're a parent and have concerns, please contact us at privacy@quizverse.com.

We may update this policy occasionally. Significant changes will be announced on our website. Your continued use of QuizVerse after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
